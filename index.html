About me

I am a PhD candidate in Financial Mathematics at The University of Sydney, supervised by Prof. Marek Rutkowski and Dr Zhou Zhou. Previously, I completed my bachelor’s studies at The University of Melbourne.

My research lies at the intersection of stochastic analysis, interest-rate modelling, and quantitative finance, with a particular focus on the pricing and hedging of backward-looking overnight interest-rate derivatives, cross-currency basis swaps and BSDEs.

I expect to submit my PhD thesis in September 2026 and am on the academic job market. I am seeking postdoctoral positions in mathematical finance, stochastic analysis and quantitative finance, and am equally keen to extend my research into machine learning, including reinforcement learning and generative AI. If you have an opportunity that may be a good fit, I would be glad to hear from you at Y.Ding@maths.usyd.edu.au.
