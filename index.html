Yining Ding

About me

I am a PhD candidate in Financial Mathematics at the University of Sydney, supervised by Professor Marek Rutkowski and Dr Zhou Zhou. My research lies at the intersection of stochastic analysis, interest-rate modelling, and quantitative finance, with a particular focus on the pricing and hedging of backward-looking overnight interest-rate derivatives, cross-currency basis swaps and BSDEs.

Research interests

  • Mathematical finance
  • Interest-rate derivatives
  • SOFR futures and backward-looking overnight rates
  • Cross-currency basis swaps
  • Stochastic analysis and BSDEs
